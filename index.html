Origonally founded in 1976 the Seattle Seahawks are one of the most iconic teams in the NFL! With three SuperBowl apperences and a victory in 2013 the Seahawks have been one of the fierces teams in the leauge since the turn of the century. The fans in Seattle are some of the most passionate and loudest in the country, with on more than one occasion triggering siezmic activity that was able to be registered as an earthquake! The team has gone through many phases but we will be focusing on a handful of teams and individuals.

Mike Holmgren was the head coach from 1999 to 2008 and lead the Seahawks to thair first Superbowl in 2006. He also oversaw the first MVP in Seahawks history with a record setting season by Shauwn Alexander carrying them to the big game. Even though he was not able to secure a championship, he helped the Hawks remain competetive while he was with the team.

Pete Carroll, hired in 2010 after a disastarous year under Jim Mora, took the reigns and with GM John Schnider brought the team back into the limelight. It did not take long for Pete to lead Seattle to a Superbowl win, a back to back apperance in the big game, and ended his carrer with the team, totallying 147 wins with his time there, easily the most in team history.

Mike MacDonald, hired directly after the departure of Carol took command of a team that had lost some of the physicallaity of earlier years under Carol and immeditly turned them around. In his first two years with the team he is the first head coach in the Seahawks history to lead them to back to back ten win seasons as the new coach of the team. Under his leadership the teams defense and identity has become a physical and dominate football team.
